FAQ's

What programs does the department offer?

We offer Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D programs in computer science and information technology.

How can i apply for admission to these programmes?

Visit our Admissions page for detailed application instructions and requirements.

What career services are available to Computer science graduates?

Our career services team provides guidance, networking opportunities, and career development support for our graduates.

Is an all science background neccessary for me to pursue this course?

A science background is not necessary for studying computer science; diverse academic backgrounds are welcome. Strong logical thinking and problem-solving skills and math, are valuable, and individuals from various fields can pursue computer science successfully.
